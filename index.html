Leadership Philosophy

I believe world‑class quality is built by empowered people, disciplined systems, and clear data. My approach combines shop‑floor coaching with visual standards and fact‑based decision making. I set simple, repeatable routines—First Output Meetings, 3 Don’ts self‑checking, CTQ/CTP controls—then develop capability so teams can sustain results without escalation. Leadership is service: remove blockers, standardize best practice, and celebrate wins.

  • People: Grow skills with OPL, targeted training, and cross‑functional problem solving.
  • Process: Stabilize with QCO Image Line, 4M input control, and standardized work.
  • Performance: Manage by visible KPIs and daily Gemba, closing gaps with rapid CAPA.
